Restore

Do you have a leaking or damaged plastic/poly water tank?

Restore services specializes in tank repairs for a range of products made from polyethylene (PE) and polypropylene (PP), or as they are usually known, “Poly”. We take pride in offering on-site tank repairs to plastic water tanks no matter what brand of the tank you have. We have the technology to repair it no matter what shape or size..

Water Tanks

We repair damaged poly/plastic water tanks, poly fuel tanks and general use poly tanks by employing a method called plastic welding

Industrial Chemical Tanks

We also repair industrial chemical poly tanks of all shapes and sizes if the plastic is a type that is suited for plastic welding.

Cost Effective

Plastic welding can save on the cost of a new water tank, transport, installation fees as well as the inconvenience of being without your water tank

Tank Installation & Plumbing

We also offer tank installation and general domestic and commercial plumbing services at cost- effective rates
